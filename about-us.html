Our Story

Decal Fixed started with a 12-year-old kid applying simple taxi stickers and a dream to master the craft. After years of supporting a family business and perfecting the art of vinyl, we officially launched into the spotlight in 2019.

While others focused on cars, Decal Fixed became the first and only expert in Cebu dedicated to custom bicycle decals. Our precision and passion for the cycling community earned us a legendary nickname among local riders: The Decal Master.

Today, Decal Fixed isn’t just a shop—it’s a hub for the Cebuano cycling culture, built on years of experience, genuine friendships, and a love for the ride.
